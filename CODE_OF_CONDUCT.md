# Code of Conduct

Harness Lab is a teaching and collaboration project. Treat the repo like a professional workshop room:

- be respectful, direct, and specific
- criticize ideas, code, and process, not people
- assume good intent, but do not ignore harmful behavior
- prefer evidence, reproduction steps, and concrete suggestions over vague dismissal
- keep participant privacy, workshop safety, and public-private boundaries in mind

## Unacceptable Behavior

This project does not accept:

- harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- doxxing, intimidation, or sexualized language
- hostile or demeaning review behavior
- sharing private workshop data, credentials, or participant information
- deliberate disruption of discussion, issues, or pull requests

## Scope

This applies to:

- issues
- pull requests
- review comments
- discussions around the project
- other project spaces controlled by the maintainers

## Reporting

If you experience or witness a serious problem, contact the maintainers privately instead of escalating it in public threads.

Use:

- [GitHub private reporting](https://github.com/ondrej-svec/harness-lab/security/advisories/new) for conduct or privacy concerns that require confidentiality
- [SUPPORT.md](SUPPORT.md) for normal help and contributor questions

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, reject contributions, or block access when behavior breaks this standard or puts participants, facilitators, or the project at risk.
